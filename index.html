About

Sharbel Najem

A professional filmmaker and commercial content creator, focused on producing high-end video content across social media, lifestyle, tourism, and brand storytelling. With a proven track record in visually rich projects, each one built around a clear strategic objective.

Every project begins with a deep understanding of the brand - its identity, audience, and goals. From creative direction and scripting to the final edit, every detail is crafted to connect with the right audience and deliver measurable impact.
